# SARS-CoV-2 Evolutionary Analysis - Kazakhstan Dataset

Phylogenetic and mutation analysis of 341 SARS-CoV-2 sequences from Kazakhstan (Aug 2021 - May 2022).
Reference: Kairov, U., Amanzhanova, A., Karabayev, D., Rakhimova, S., Aitkulova, A., Samatkyzy, D., Kalendar, R., Kozhamkulov, U., Molkenov, A., Gabdulkayum, A., Sarbassov, D., & Akilzhanova, A. (2022). A high scale SARS-CoV-2 profiling by its whole-genome sequencing using Oxford Nanopore Technology in Kazakhstan. Frontiers in Genetics, 13, 906318. https://doi.org/10.3389/fgene.2022.906318
